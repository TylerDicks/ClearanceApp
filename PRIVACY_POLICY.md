# Privacy Policy

**Clearance – Aviation Weather App**  
**Effective Date:** April 19, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

---

## Overview

Clearance is a personal weather assessment tool for pilots and aviation professionals. It does not require an account, does not transmit personally identifiable information to any developer server, and does not display advertising. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

---

## Location Data

Clearance requests access to your device's GPS location solely to retrieve local weather conditions relevant to your position. Your coordinates are sent directly to third-party weather APIs (listed below) over HTTPS each time you request a weather update.

- Location data is **never stored on any developer server**.
- Location data is **not shared** with any party beyond the API requests described in this policy.
- You may deny location permission; however, the App's core weather-fetching functionality will not be available without it.

---

## Third-Party Data Sources

The following services receive your location coordinates to return weather and aviation data:

| Service | Data Provided | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Open-Meteo** (open-meteo.com) | Weather forecasts (temperature, wind, precipitation, UV Index, etc.) | open-meteo.com/en/terms |
| **Aviation Weather Center** (aviationweather.gov) | METAR & TAF reports | aviationweather.gov/docs/about |
| **NOAA Space Weather Prediction Center** (swpc.noaa.gov) | Kp Index (geomagnetic activity) | nws.noaa.gov/privacy.shtml |

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. Clearance does not control how these services handle data once a request is made.

---

## Subscription Data (Clearance Pro)

If you purchase a Clearance Pro subscription, your purchase is processed through the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Subscription entitlement verification is handled by **RevenueCat, Inc.**

- RevenueCat may assign an anonymous customer identifier to your device to track subscription status.
- Clearance does not receive or store your payment card information at any point.
- RevenueCat may collect certain device and usage data in accordance with its own privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.
- No personally identifiable information beyond what RevenueCat or the App Stores collect for transaction processing is shared with any developer server.

---

## Local Storage

Your threshold preferences, unit settings (Imperial/Metric), custom weather condition mappings, and cached weather data are stored locally on your device using standard system storage (AsyncStorage). This data:

- **Never leaves your device** and is not accessible to us.
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the App.

---

## No Tracking or Analytics

Clearance does not use analytics SDKs, crash reporters, session recorders, or advertising frameworks. Specifically:

- No advertising identifiers (IDFA, GAID, or device fingerprints) are collected or transmitted.
- No behavioral or usage analytics are sent to any server.
- No third-party advertising networks are integrated.

---

## Children's Privacy

Clearance does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). The App does not collect personal information of any kind beyond the location access described above, which is used solely for weather retrieval. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through the App, please contact us at the address below.

---

## Data Security

All network requests made by the App (to weather APIs and RevenueCat) are transmitted over HTTPS. Because Clearance does not operate its own servers or databases, there is no developer-side storage of your data that could be subject to a server-side breach. Preferences stored locally are protected by your device's built-in security mechanisms.

## Your Choices

| Control | How |
|---|---|
| Revoke location access | Device Settings → Privacy → Location Services → Clearance |
| Delete locally stored preferences | Uninstall the App |

| Restore prior purchases | "Restore Purchases" option in the App |
